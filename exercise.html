Aerobics

Aerobics is a kind of physical exercise for increasing the stamina and improves lung's functioning. It is a kind of dance form which reduces the health risk of a person.

Some examples of aerobic exercise includes:

  • Running
  • Cycling
  • Walking
  • Swimming
  • Aerobic classes

Aerobic exercise benefits the body in many different ways.

  • Maintaining a healthy weight.
  • Controlling blood sugar levels.
  • Lowering blood pressure.
  • Increasing lifespan.
  • Improving physical functioning.

National physical activity guidelines recommend at least 30 minutes of moderate intensity or 50 minutes of high intensity aerobic activity every day.

People wishing to lose weight will need to ensure that they burn more calories than they consume,resulting in a caloric deficit.

Yoga

Yoga is a method of exercise in which to increase the relaxation and bringing a peaceful mind.

Yoga has began as a spiritual practice but has become very famous as a way of promoting physical and mental well-being.

There are many different yoga styles ranging from simple and gentle practices to physically demanding ones.

Basically,yoga helps to improve general wellness by relieving stress and anxiety,supporting good habits,better sleep cycle and also improving mental or emotional health.

Yoga can control several pains of the body such as back pain,neck pain etc.

There's evidence that yoga may help people to lose weight.
