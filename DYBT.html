Which is your body type

Determine your body type

For analysing which exercise or diet will workout for your body type !!
Yes ! You are on the right page!!
Different body types=different life style,different routines,different food habits,different culture!!Everyone is different !

There are mainly 3 different body types:

Ectomorph

If you have a ectomorph body type,then you will find very hard to gain muscles as well as fat.To help with this problem,try focusing on the compound movements than isolated movements.It will take more time for you to gain your desired goals, but don't worry you can consume food more than other body types.Anyhow, eat right amount of food for your health.

Some characteristics of this body type are:

  • Lean physique.
  • Delicate frame.
  • Body similar to a marathon runner.
  • Fast metabolism.

Pro-tips:

*It is best to stick to complex carbs that can leave you feeling fuller for longer.

*Supplements such as protein shakes and BCAAs could give you energy for extra boost.

*Don't over train yourself for more than 1 hour.

Endomorph

People with a wider and round body are basically called Endomorph body type.They may have characteristics and traits that make it difficult for them to workout or diet.These type of persons may need to carefully control what they consume.These people usually carry excess weight. Gaining muscles are also sometimes difficult for these people. But don't worry, you can acheive your goals by proper planning of workout and diet.

Some characteristics of this body type are:

  • Round and soft physique with wider waist and chest.
  • Has the best strength when compared to other body types.
  • Has more fat.
  • Slow metabolism.

Pro-tips:

*It is best to stick to aerobic exercises and also focusing on HIIT(High Intensity Workouts).

*You should train your overall body to see results and never focus on only one area.

*Don't over the amount you consume.It is preferrable to control what you eat in a day.

Mesomorph

In between ectomorph and endomorph is the mesomorph body type. They are the luckiest body type of all the three.Because, they can gain and loss weight very easily.They do not have to go very insanely into exercises and diet controls.

Some characteristics of this body type are:

  • Can be lean and muscular.
  • Have well defined muscles.
  • Middle of the body types.
  • Highest immunity.

Pro-tips:

*They can do any type of exercises.But better to include aerobic exercises.

*Supplements equal amount of protein and fat, with a moderate quantity of carbs.

*Focus on complex carbs to give your body more energy.

Now,What's your body type?
